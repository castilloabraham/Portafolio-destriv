Own Portfolio

Figma / HTML & CSS / JavaScripts

When you start in the developing world is vital to have a personal portfolio, where you can show your skills through projects you have done, but although it is quite obviously not all developers do it, closing to some opportunities.

Many times I showed as portfolio a landing page that I had developed at the beginning of my career as a programmer, but as time went by this was not professional and did not really show everything I was capable of; after learning Figma and making several test designs I decided to create what would be my new portfolio where I really show who I am and what my skills are.

Currently, you are browsing my portfolio which is a project under construction, which I will be updating and improving little by little.

It was designed in Figma and developed in HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
